Pasta Carbonara

Ingredient Amount
spaghetti 350 g
pancetta 100 g
large eggs 3
parmesan 100 g
  1. Cook the pasta in plenty of salted water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, cut the guanciale, pancetta or bacon into small cubes or strips. Fry in a moderately hot pan with a little olive oil until golden and crispy.
  3. Place the egg (yolk) in a large, room-warm bowl in which you plan to serve the pasta dish. Beat the eggs with a whisk. Stir in the grated cheese, season generously with black pepper. Now whisk in a good ladle of the hot pasta cooking liquid!*
  4. Then drain the spaghetti and add it immediately to the egg-cheese mixture. Stir quickly, stir in the (drained) meat cubes if necessary. Sprinkle with more grated cheese and black pepper and serve immediately.
